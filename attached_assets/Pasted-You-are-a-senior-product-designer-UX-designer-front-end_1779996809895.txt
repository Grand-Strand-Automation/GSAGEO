## Calculator scoring logic
Implement simple, credible scoring logic.
Do NOT use fake precision.

### A. Current spend
Calculate:
- currentMonthlySpend = sum of all monthly cost fields
- currentAnnualSpend = currentMonthlySpend * 12

### B. Overlap score
Create a weighted overlap score based on answers such as:
- duplicate security tools
- duplicate backup tools
- multiple vendors
- uncertainty about tool usage
- unused licenses
- manual onboarding and offboarding

Return a label:
- Low
- Moderate
- High

### C. Compliance and control score
Create a weighted risk score based on:
- no multi-factor authentication
- undocumented onboarding
- undocumented offboarding
- unclear backup ownership
- no restore testing
- weak access review controls
- Microsoft 365 administration uncertainty

Return a label:
- Strong baseline
- Needs review
- Priority gap

### D. Savings range
Use a realistic range, not a fake exact number.

Example logic:
- base likely savings opportunity starts at 8 percent of monthly spend for low-overlap environments
- moderate overlap environments can show 12 to 20 percent
- high-overlap environments can show 18 to 30 percent
- add modest range adjustments if licensing confusion, duplicate tools, or manual administrative processes are present

Display as:
- Estimated potential monthly savings: $X to $Y
- Estimated potential annual savings: $A to $B

Do not overpromise.
Use clear language like:
- estimated savings opportunity
- likely areas of overlap
- areas worth reviewing
