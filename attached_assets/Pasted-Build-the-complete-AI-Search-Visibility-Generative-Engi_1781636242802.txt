PHASE 5 — BUILD THE AUTOMATED GEO AUDIT WORKFLOW
==================================================

Build a practical v1 audit pipeline triggered by GEO intake submissions.

Workflow:
1. save submission
2. create audit job
3. set status to queued
4. process job
5. store results
6. set status complete or failed

Audit modules required for v1:

A. PAGE DISCOVERY
- fetch homepage
- try sitemap.xml
- try robots.txt
- gather key public page URLs if discoverable

B. TECHNICAL CHECKS
- homepage status
- sitemap presence
- robots presence
- title/meta extraction
- canonical presence if detectable
- structured data presence if detectable
- basic crawlability findings

C. ENTITY / BUSINESS EXTRACTION
Try to extract:
- business name
- contact information
- service labels
- location mentions
- visible positioning clues

D. CONTENT ARCHITECTURE CHECKS
Look for presence or likely absence of:
- home
- about
- services
- contact
- FAQ
- pricing
- process
- case studies / testimonials
- service pages
- educational pages
- trust / proof pages

E. GEO CONTENT GAP CHECKS
Rules-based suggestions for missing assets such as:
- service pages
- city/service pages where appropriate
- FAQ hubs
- comparison pages
- pricing/process pages
- case studies
- checklist or guide pages
- about/founder/trust pages

F. INTERNAL STRUCTURE SIGNALS
Where possible:
- whether key pages appear linked
- whether structure seems shallow or fragmented
- whether navigable architecture exists

G. SCORECARD
Generate category scores for:
- crawlability
- technical readiness
- entity clarity
- service-page depth
- trust / proof content
- answer-engine content coverage
- internal structure
- conversion readiness

H. RECOMMENDATION ENGINE
Generate:
- top 5 priority fixes
- top 5 content opportunities
- suggested next pages to build
- recommended offer tier:
  - Audit only
  - Foundation
  - Growth

I. HUMAN-READABLE SUMMARY
Generate a readable summary explaining:
- what was found
- biggest gaps
- what to do next

Keep the output practical and explainable. If uncertain, mark uncertainty clearly.
